Processors

Processors transform incoming REAPER track audio. They do not assume an ambisonic format. Use Mix / Pan for routing, fold-down, metering, bus mixing, and direct speaker panning. Use Ambisonics for ACN/SN3D and 3OAFX tools.

Topology Processors

  • Delay Processor 8ch/24ch: topology-driven multichannel delay and the best starting point for topology controls.
  • Wave Geometry Processor 8ch: non-FFT topology processor for fold, clip, sample-step, bit-collapse, scope, and tape-head loop behavior.
  • Spectral Topology Processor 8ch/24ch: topology-shaped spectral processing with lane patching and a grayscale sonogram view.

Macro Processors

  • Macro Delay 8ch/24ch: compact multichannel delay with shared engine controls and lane relationship controls.
  • Macro Pitch 8ch/24ch: compact multichannel pitch shifting using the shared Macro layout.
  • Macro Shred Mono/8ch/24ch: pressure, wavefolding, and bounded resonant feedback for voice and microphone input. Tune follows a smoothed input spectral centroid, while the multichannel builds add lane relationship controls.

Other Processors

  • Spectral Spray 2ch/8ch: FFT-bin scatter, smear, hold, freeze, holes, phase blur, feedback, and frequency-window effects.
  • Buffer Processor 8ch: skip, reverse, crush, error-path, and corrupted-window experiments.
  • Shard Scatter: 2-in/16-out grain-shard spatial scatter.
  • Orbit Delay: 2-in/16-out orbiting delay with transport-stop de-click behavior.
  • Cascade Taps: 2-in/16-out stepped tap-ring processor with a soft handoff control.
