Ambisonic Effects

Ambisonic effects transform a complete encoded field while preserving the relationships among its channels. Place these after an encoder or ambisonic instrument and before the final decoder.

Field Effects

  • 3OAFX Effects: third-order Delay, Filter, Gain, and Pitch inserts with an internal 24-point virtual-speaker layer.
  • 3OAFX Displacement: time-varying third-order field warps loaded from Displacement Score geometry.
  • Ambi Imprint: directional convolution using .s3gimprint space responses.
  • Ambi Rotate: coherent first-through-seventh-order field rotation.

Field and Bus Effects

  • Ambi Depth: depth, focus, air, tail, low-body, and width shaping for one third-order field.
  • Ambi Field / Bus Effects: group matrix, node mixing, rotation, and depth across lane-locked third-order feeds.
  • Ambi Grain Processor: loaded-media granulation with one shared grain event stream across all active ambisonic channels.

Signal-chain Rule

encoder or encoded media
-> ambisonic effects
-> decoder
-> speaker or stereo output

Do not treat individual ambisonic channels as unrelated stems. Use the field-aware processors here when timing, filtering, geometry, or gain changes must remain coherent across the encoding.
