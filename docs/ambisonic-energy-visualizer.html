Ambi Energy Visualizer 64

s3g Ambi Energy Visualizer 64 is a fixed 64-in/64-out passthrough analyzer for ACN/SN3D ambisonic signals up to 7OA. It does not alter the audio. It reconstructs a high-resolution rectangular view of energy across the sphere.

What It Shows

The display evaluates synchronized HOA snapshots over a 256 x 128 grid of virtual probe directions. This preserves relationships between ambisonic channels instead of treating each channel as an independent meter.

  • Body uses orders 0-2 to keep broad and diffuse field energy visible.
  • Detail uses the full 7OA field to reveal tighter directional structure.
  • Wake leaves a short inverse-color afterimage behind receding energy.

A first-order direction estimate guides contrast and wake movement; it does not add energy to the map. The result is an interpretive field monitor for concentration, spread, and motion, not a calibrated SPL or localization measurement.

For raw channel routing, use Multichannel Meter 64. For speaker output after decoding, use the MAP page in Ambi Speaker Decoder 64.

Controls

  • MAP chooses between FIELD, BLUE, INK, VOLT, CLASSIC, INFERNO, VIRIDIS, and MAGMA. It changes only the display.
  • SIZE selects NORMAL or LARGE. LARGE asks REAPER to expand the embedded GUI near the available screen size; NORMAL restores the default 980 x 560 view.

The CLAP plugin controls its canvas size, while REAPER owns the surrounding FX window. For an OS-level fullscreen view, float or maximize that REAPER window after selecting LARGE.

Routing

The plugin exposes one 64-channel input bus and one 64-channel output bus. Audio passes through channel-for-channel, and unused output lanes are cleared. Insert it after an ambisonic encoder, after an ambisonic processor, or before a speaker decoder to inspect field behavior without changing the chain.

Field analysis and GPU drawing run only while the custom GUI is open. Closing the window leaves the channel-for-channel passthrough active without visual-analysis work.
