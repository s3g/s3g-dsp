Ambi Energy Visualizer 64

s3g Ambi Energy Visualizer 64 is a fixed 64-in/64-out passthrough analyzer for ACN/SN3D ambisonic signals. It does not decode or alter audio. It estimates where energy appears in the ambisonic field and draws that as a high-resolution flattened heatmap.

input1OA..7OA
formatACN/SN3D
routing64 in / 64 out
audiopassthrough

What It Shows

The heatmap is not a speaker meter. It reads the ambisonic channel energy for the selected order, evaluates the ambisonic basis over a flattened sphere, and colors the map by estimated field energy. Use it to see broad directional concentration, motion, and spread in an ambisonic chain.

For raw channel routing, use Multichannel Meter 64. For speaker output after decoding, use the MAP page in Ambi Speaker Decoder 64.

Controls

  • MAP chooses the visual color map. The current visual assumes a 7OA-capable 64-channel input shape and emphasizes readable field motion over scientific measurement.

Routing

The plugin exposes one 64-channel input bus and one 64-channel output bus. Audio passes through channel-for-channel, and unused output lanes are cleared. Insert it after an ambisonic encoder, after an ambisonic processor, or before a speaker decoder to inspect field behavior without changing the chain.
