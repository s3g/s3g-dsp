Ambi Wind Encoder

s3g Ambi Wind Encoder 64 is a zero-input instrument that extends the filtered-noise wind patch into a moving field of as many as 64 voices. Gusts, turbulent modulation, resonant materials, microweather motion, and an environment field are encoded directly to first-through-seventh-order ACN/SN3D.

Workflow

  1. Insert the plugin on a 64-channel REAPER track.
  2. Choose a factory preset, then set ORDER and VOICES.
  3. Balance the continuous wind layer, gust shape, and material response.
  4. Shape microweather motion and place the field with direction, elevation, range, and inertia.
  5. Follow the instrument with an ambisonic decoder or meter.

Wind, Gust, and Material

  • WIND sets the continuous noise body. GUST RATE, GUST DEPTH, GUST SHAPE, and GUST EDGE control the pressure envelopes layered over it.
  • TURBULENCE, FLUTTER, and VECTOR LFO add irregular modulation at different time scales.
  • MATERIAL TYPE chooses OPEN, LEAF, HOLLOW, WIRE, METAL, CHIMES, BLOCKS, HARP, REEDS, or FABRIC. MATERIAL sets how strongly that resonant or impact layer participates.
  • CENTER, SWEEP, Q, SHRILL, BODY, BREATH, GRIT, AIR, and HISS shape the filtered spectrum and nonlinear surface detail.

Microweather Motion

FIELD RATE, FLOW PUSH, SHEAR, CURL, and UPDRAFT move voice parcels through the field. DEPTH PUSH expands their radial motion. DIRECTION, ELEVATION, and RANGE set the macro vector; INERTIA controls how smoothly the encoded positions follow it.

Environment Field

PLACE selects OPEN, CANOPY, PORCH, ROOM, HANGAR, CANYON, or TUNNEL. ENV RETURN, ENV SIZE, ENV DECAY, and ENV DAMPING mix and shape the corresponding encoded environment response.

Presets

The 22 factory presets range from the restrained PAIA Bench Wind reference patch to tree-line, architectural, storm, material, and aeolian scenes. SAVE and LOAD write portable .s3gwind presets, with the default folder at ~/Music/s3g/Presets/Ambi Wind Encoder/.

Order and Routing

The output bus remains 64 channels wide. ORDER activates the corresponding ACN channels and clears the rest.
