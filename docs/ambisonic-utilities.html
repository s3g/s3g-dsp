Ambisonic Utilities

Utilities inspect, reshape, and exchange ambisonic material without acting as a source encoder or final listening decoder. They are useful for checking order, visualizing energy, and moving authored spatial data between s3g-mc and s3g-dsp.

Inspection and Order

  • Ambi Order / Band: solos, mutes, or trims complete ambisonic orders and channel bands.
  • Ambi Energy Visualizer: passthrough direction, order, and energy inspection for as many as 64 channels.

Interchange Formats

  • .s3gimprint format: directional space-response data exported by Imprint Sketch and loaded by Ambi Imprint.
  • .s3gray format: spatial Ray field data exported by Ray Sketch and loaded by the Ray encoders.

Format Baseline

The ambisonic plugins use ACN channel order and SN3D normalization. A complete order contains (order + 1)^2 channels: 4 for 1OA, 9 for 2OA, 16 for 3OA, and 64 for 7OA. Confirm that convention before diagnosing a decoder or visualizer as incorrect.
