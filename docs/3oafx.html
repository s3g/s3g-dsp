Ambisonics

Ambisonics pages cover plugins and infrastructure whose channel order, normalization, and host routing are part of the signal model. The section is organized by the job each tool performs instead of one growing plugin list.

The supported target is REAPER on macOS. These plugins use ACN/SN3D ambisonics and fixed-width buses where predictable routing matters.

Sections

  • Encoders: turn ordinary audio into an ambisonic field or generate an encoded field directly.
  • Decoders: render a field to speaker layouts, object and sub feeds, stereo, or headphones.
  • Effects: transform complete encoded fields, including 3OAFX, displacement, convolution, rotation, depth, bus, and granular tools.
  • Utilities: inspect orders and energy or work with the .s3gimprint and .s3gray interchange formats.

Processing Flow

audio-input encoder or ambisonic instrument
-> optional ambisonic effects
-> optional order and energy utilities
-> speaker, stereo, or head decoder

A complete order contains (order + 1)^2 channels. Set the REAPER track width before inserting a plugin, keep the order and ACN/SN3D convention consistent through the chain, and decode only when the field reaches its listening destination.

Direct speaker panners are documented under Direct Panning. They share layout language with the ambisonic tools, but they do not encode or decode an ambisonic stream.
