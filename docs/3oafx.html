Ambisonics

Ambisonics pages cover ambisonics-specific plugins and infrastructure. These tools are separate from the general multichannel effects and instruments because channel order, spatial format, and host routing are part of the instrument.

The supported target is REAPER on macOS, using ACN/SN3D ambisonics and fixed-width buses where predictable routing matters. The point encoder and 3OAFX effects are third-order; the VOT, cloud, and path encoders, decoders, and utilities support first through seventh order where noted.

Plugin Areas

  • 3OAFX single-effect processors: Delay, Filter, Gain, and Pitch for third-order inserts with an internal 24-point virtual speaker layer.
  • Ambi Point Encoder: point-source AED/gain automation into third-order ACN/SN3D.
  • Ambi Cloud Encoder: up to 64 source lanes distributed into one to four first-through-seventh-order ambisonic clouds.
  • Ambi Path Encoder: up to 64 source lanes encoded along saved, imported, or drawn path trajectories.
  • Ambi VOT Encoder: 64-voice vector-wavetable instrument with composed U/V scoring and first-through-seventh-order ACN/SN3D output.
  • Ambi Decoder family: Speaker, Object, Adaptive, and Sub decoders for speaker layouts, object-focused paths, adaptive decode behavior, and low-frequency sub feeds.
  • Ambi Rotate, Ambi Depth, and Ambi Order / Band: field utilities for encoded material.
  • Ambi Field / Bus Processors: group matrix, node, rotate, and depth tools for lane-locked 3OA feeds.
  • Ambi Stereo Decoder, Ambi Head Decoder, and Ambi Energy Visualizer: monitoring and visualization tools.
  • Ambi Grain Processor: loaded-media ambisonic instrument with channel-locked grain events.

Direct speaker panners are documented under Mix / Pan. They share layout language with the ambisonic tools, but they do not encode or decode an ambisonic stream.

3OAFX Processing Flow

Current CLAP 3OAFX effects are ordinary third-order ACN/SN3D inserts:

3OA source
-> s3g 3OAFX Delay, Filter, Gain, or Pitch
-> optional additional 3OAFX effects
-> Ambi Speaker Decoder, Ambi Stereo Decoder, or Ambi Head Decoder

Inside each effect, dry and wet paths stay separate until the masked mixer stage. Stack separate plugin instances in series when order matters. For parallel combinations, use REAPER routing.
