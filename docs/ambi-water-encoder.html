Ambi Water Encoder

s3g Ambi Water Encoder 64 is a zero-input procedural instrument for currents, rain, cascades, surges, vortices, sloshes, drips, and bubble plumes. As many as 64 moving parcels combine flow noise with discrete droplet, splash, and bubble events, then encode the result directly to first-through-seventh-order ACN/SN3D.

Procedural water synthesis combines a continuous noise body with short modeled events. Droplets and splashes supply impacts, while bubble size controls resonant pitch and decay; no water recordings are played.

Workflow

  1. Insert the plugin on a 64-channel REAPER track.
  2. Choose a factory preset, then set ORDER and VOICES.
  3. Select a water regime and contact environment.
  4. Balance continuous flow against drops, splashes, and bubbles.
  5. Shape parcel motion and the encoded environment, then follow the instrument with a decoder or meter.

Water Source

WATER REGIME selects CURRENT, RAIN, CASCADE, SURGE, VORTEX, SLOSH, DRIP, or BUBBLE PLUME. ENVIRONMENT changes the contact material among OPEN, ROCK, LEAVES, MUD, CONCRETE, METAL, GLASS, PIPE, and CAVE.

WATER, FLOW, SCALE, TURBULENCE, and AERATION establish the continuous body and the conditions from which discrete events emerge.

Events and Body

  • DROPS, SPLASH, and BUBBLES set the three event layers; EVENT DENSITY, EVENT SIZE, and EVENT LIFE control their population and duration.
  • The event model uses radius-dependent bubble resonance, damping, rise, and impact scaling as synthesis constraints rather than playing water recordings.
  • DEPTH, BRIGHTNESS, BUBBLE RISE, DAMPING, and IMPACT TEXTURE balance submerged body, surface detail, and contact response.

Parcel Motion

PARCEL RATE, CURRENT, SLOPE, EDDY, CONVERGENCE, and WIDTH define the large-scale trajectory. SPREAD and DEVIATION separate the voices. DIRECTION, ELEVATION, RANGE, and INERTIA place and smooth the resulting field.

Environment Field

PLACE selects OPEN, SUBMERGED, CAVE, CISTERN, CHANNEL, or PIPE. ENV RETURN, ENV SIZE, ENV DECAY, and ENV DAMPING mix and shape the encoded environment response independently of the contact material.

LISTEN lets the generated ambisonic environment affect what happens next. Eight virtual directional pickups decode the combined direct water field and Environment Field return; their slowly changing energy envelopes give the synthesis loop directional memory. FOLLOW draws current and eddies toward accumulated energy, COUNTER steers toward its opposite, and BALANCE favors less occupied directions. The same directional score also biases turbulence and droplet, splash, and bubble event density. Motion is interpolated continuously rather than jumping between pickup directions. OFF—the factory-preset default—keeps the original open-loop behavior while the pickups continue warming their memory.

Presets

The 16 factory presets include creeks, rain surfaces, spillways, drips, slosh, vortices, waterfalls, bubbles, rapids, runoff, storms, and still water. SAVE and LOAD write portable .s3gwater presets, including the listener mode, with the default folder at ~/Music/s3g/Presets/Ambi Water Encoder/. RANDOM also chooses a listener mode, so a randomized scene may immediately become self-steering.
