Multichannel

Multichannel covers effects and routing tools whose channels are ordinary audio lanes or physical speaker feeds. Unlike Ambisonics, these plugins do not treat the bus as an ACN/SN3D sound field.

Sections

  • Effects: topology, macro, spectral, buffer, and distributed-output processing.
  • Monitoring / Fold-down: stereo and quad audition paths plus fixed-width multichannel metering.
  • Direct Panning: DBAP, LBAP, VBAP, and layout editing for source-to-speaker placement.
  • Bus Mixing / Routing: group matrices, wide node buses, and channel-path validation.
  • Speaker-array Utilities: layout-aware subwoofer crossover and per-output calibration.

Choose a Path

Use Effects when the channels themselves are the material being transformed. Use Monitoring / Fold-down to hear or inspect an existing bus, Direct Panning when source signals should be placed onto physical outputs, Bus Mixing / Routing when complete channel groups must be combined, and Speaker-array Utilities for the final per-speaker correction stage.

Ambisonic or Direct?

These families work with ordinary channel lanes or physical speaker feeds. Use Ambisonics when the bus itself is an ACN/SN3D sound field, including Ambi Field / Bus Effects when each source block is a complete third-order feed.
