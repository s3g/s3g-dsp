Ambisonic Encoders

Encoders create an ACN/SN3D sound field from ordinary source lanes or generate an encoded field directly. Choose an audio-input encoder when a REAPER source already exists; choose a spatial instrument when the plugin itself is the sound source.

Encoder editors retain their native control geometry inside a responsive viewport. A host may resize the window down to 480 by 360 pixels; horizontal and vertical scrollers expose the complete editor without shrinking its controls or changing point-field hit testing. Moving an editor to a smaller display requests a screen-fitting viewport automatically.

Audio-input Encoders

  • Ambi Point Encoder: 1-to-64-lane point-source AED and gain automation into selectable first- through seventh-order ambisonics.
  • Ambi Cloud Encoder: distributes as many as 64 source lanes into one to four first-through-seventh-order clouds.
  • Ambi Terrain Navigator: moves source-lane coordinates across nested procedural terrain spheres.
  • Ambi Path Encoder: follows saved, imported, or hand-drawn spatial trajectories.
  • Ambi Ray Encoder: places a mono source and listener inside a direct, early-reflection, and late-room field.
  • Ambi Ray Bilocation Encoder: runs two Ray fields continuously and moves a mono source through their overlap.

Spatial Instruments

  • Ambi VOT Encoder: interpolates among sixteen waveforms while voices follow editable paths through the resulting timbre field.
  • Ambi Vox Encoder: separates voice recordings into pitch, spectral, and noise components for phrase-based resynthesis and ensemble motion.
  • Ambi Wave Terrain Encoder: turns closed contour scans across a procedural surface into oscillator waveforms and spatial positions.
  • Ambi Wind Encoder: shapes broadband noise into gusts, turbulent air, and resonant material responses.
  • Ambi Water Encoder: combines continuous flow noise with modeled droplets, splashes, and resonant bubbles.
  • Ambi Insect Encoder: builds stridulating, tymbal, wingbeat, and mixed populations with coupled call clocks and swarm motion.
  • Ambi Pulsar Encoder: repeats short windowed waveforms from rhythmic rates into pitch, with recurrent-network waveform capture.
  • Ambi Neural Ecology: grows four to sixty-four recurrent nodes whose adaptive four/eight-pickup auditory field navigates a Hunt/Tudor-referenced lattice score.
  • Ambi Stochastic Encoder: uses second-order random walks to evolve breakpoint amplitude and duration curves.
  • Ambi Wrangler Encoder: couples oscillator pairs through comparator-driven feedback shift registers for repeating but unstable patterns.

Routing Baseline

Set the REAPER track width before inserting an encoder. The 64-channel encoders use the first (order + 1)^2 channels and silence the unused higher-order channels. Follow the encoder with a tool from Decoders, Effects, or Utilities.
