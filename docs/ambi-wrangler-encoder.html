Ambi Wrangler Encoder

s3g Ambi Wrangler Encoder 64 is an autonomous Benjolin/rungler-style logic instrument with as many as 64 voices and direct first-through-seventh-order ACN/SN3D output. It has no audio input or MIDI note input; use its parameters and factory states to shape a continuously evolving field.

Workflow

  1. Insert the plugin on a 64-channel REAPER track.
  2. Choose a factory state, then set the active voice count and ambisonic order.
  3. Shape oscillator coupling, rungler behavior, filtering, and saturation.
  4. Set topology motion and the field's AED center.
  5. Follow the instrument with an ambisonic decoder or meter.

Factory States

The PRESET parameter recalls a complete starting state. Available states are Backwash Logic, Glass Register, Low Rope, Square Weather, Filter Bloom, Narrow Organism, Upper Sparks, and Fault Lattice.

Logic Synthesis

  • BASE sets the central oscillator note; SPREAD distributes voices in semitones and DETUNE adds stable per-voice variation.
  • CHAOS and CROSS set instability and coupling between each voice's oscillator pair.
  • RUNG, RUNG SIZE, and THRESHOLD shape the shift-register feedback and comparator behavior.
  • COLOR, FILTER, and RESONANCE set the state-variable filter response.
  • SATURATION increases bounded nonlinear drive.

Topology and Placement

TOPOLOGY selects the shared spatial shape. MOTION offers STATIC, ORBIT, DRIFT, FOLD, and STIR. Motion rate, amount, depth, scale, and collapse reshape the field while it runs.

AZIMUTH, ELEVATION, and DISTANCE place the field center. SPATIAL FOLLOW controls how fully each voice follows its topology point instead of collapsing toward that center.

Order and Routing

The output bus remains 64 channels wide. ORDER activates 4, 9, 16, 25, 36, 49, or 64 channels for first through seventh order and clears the rest. Follow it with an Ambi Speaker Decoder, Ambi Stereo Decoder, Ambi Head Decoder, or Ambi Energy Visualizer.
