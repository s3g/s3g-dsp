Instruments

Instruments generate sound internally, respond to MIDI, or use loaded audio as their source. Spatial instruments also appear under Ambisonic Encoders; Ambi Grain Processor appears under Ambisonic Effects because its loaded media remains an encoded field.

Sections

  • Generative Instruments: internally synthesized byte, terrain, weather, liquid, pulsaret, stochastic, and logic fields.
  • Sample / Loop Instruments: one or more loaded files used as persistent multichannel or encoded source material.
  • Voice / Language Instruments: voicebank synthesis, phrase mapping, and the companion voicebank-building workflow.

Cross-listed Spatial Instruments

Spatial instruments are listed here by how their sound is generated and under Ambisonic Encoders by signal format. The instrument pages explain synthesis and performance; the Ambisonics sections provide the surrounding encoder, effect, utility, and decoder workflow.

Routing

Check the instrument's output format before inserting it. Fault and the loop instruments use ordinary multichannel lanes. Ambisonic instruments use a fixed 64-channel bus with the active order occupying the first (order + 1)^2 channels.
