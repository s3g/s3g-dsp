Instruments

Instruments generate sound internally, respond to MIDI, or use loaded audio as their source. Loop Processor and Multi Loop Processor live here because they are CLAP instrument plugins. Ambi Grain Processor is also a CLAP instrument, with its main reference under Ambisonics because it assumes encoded media.
