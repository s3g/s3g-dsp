Instruments

Instruments generate sound internally, respond to MIDI, or use loaded audio as their source. Ambisonic instruments also appear in the Ambisonics section when their routing needs additional context.

Instrument Pages

  • Loop Processor: 8-channel loaded-audio loop instrument.
  • Multi Loop Processor: 8-channel instrument for up to four loaded files.
  • Ambi VOT Encoder: 64-voice vector-wavetable instrument with composed timbre paths and first-through-seventh-order ACN/SN3D output.
  • Ambi Vox Encoder: 64-voice vocal vector-wavetable instrument with phrase articulation and Black Metal, Throat, Choir, and Animal VOX atlas sources.
  • Ambi Wave Terrain Encoder: 64-voice stochastic wave-terrain instrument with contour interpolation and first-through-seventh-order ACN/SN3D output.
  • Ambi Stochastic Encoder: 64-voice instrument with second-order pressure walks, generator selection, time-fields, topology-driven AED placement, and first-through-seventh-order ACN/SN3D output.
  • Ambi Grain Processor: 16-channel instrument for loaded ACN/SN3D media with channel-locked grain events.
